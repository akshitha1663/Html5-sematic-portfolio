About Me

I design for people first and pixels second.

I am a front-end developer focused on creating calm, accessible interfaces that help businesses connect with their audience.

My work blends modern HTML, thoughtful CSS, and a strong commitment to usability. I believe good websites should feel intuitive, inclusive, and easy to navigate.
